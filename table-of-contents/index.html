What is Minion Rush?

Minion Rush is a fun-filled, family-friendly endless runner game created by Gameloft in collaboration with Illumination and Universal. In Minion Rush, you can play as either Dave, Jerry, Carl, or any of the other various minions from the Despicable Me and Minions movies! You can also traverse through some of the most famous locations of the minions universe! With incredible graphics and intriguing swiping and tilting mechanisms, Minion Rush is an award-winning game and among one of the most popular endless running games in the mobile market.

Similar to games like Subway Surfers, Minion Rush offers a wide selection of characters and costumes that you can unlock in many different ways. The main premise of the game is to run and complete missions. These missions can award you with costume cards that can be used to unlock specific costumes. Each costume gives you a particular buff per run. You can upgrade the costume’s buffs by collecting additional costume cards and banana coins.
